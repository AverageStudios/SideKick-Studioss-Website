Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the questions we get most often.

How do I access my SideKick Studios dashboard?+
Log in at app.sidekickstudioss.com using the email and password you signed up with. Once inside, your dashboard gives you access to your campaigns, CRM, analytics, and all your marketing tools.
What's the first thing I should do after signing up?+
Log into your dashboard and complete your business profile. Then explore the campaign templates — pick one that fits your industry and launch your first ad. The onboarding checklist inside will walk you through every step.
How do I add leads and contacts to my CRM?+
Add contacts manually, import a CSV file, or connect your lead capture forms so contacts are added automatically. Your CRM syncs with your campaigns so every lead is tracked from first click to closed deal.
How do I track my campaign performance?+
Head to your dashboard and click Analytics in the left sidebar. You'll find a full breakdown — open rates, click-through rates, leads generated, and more. Check this weekly to spot trends.
How do I set up my automated follow-up campaigns?+
In your dashboard, go to Automations and select a follow-up sequence template. Customize the timing, messages, and triggers. Need help? Reach out to support and we'll walk you through it live.
