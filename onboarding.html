Step-by-Step

Getting Started in 4 Steps

Follow these and you'll be live and capturing leads within the hour.

Step 01
📩

Check Your Email & Set Your Password

Right after purchase, SideKick Studioss sent you a welcome email with your login link and a temporary password. Open it, click the link, and create your new password.

Can't find it? Check your spam folder.

Step 02
🖥️

Log Into Your Dashboard

Once your password is set, head to app.sidekickstudioss.com anytime. Everything is pre-built — your funnels, automations, and pipeline are already waiting for you.

Step 03
📣

Launch Your First Ad

Go to the Ad Templates section inside your dashboard. Pick the template built for your industry, connect your Facebook/Meta account, and go live. Our ads are tested and proven — no guesswork needed.

Step 04
📊

Watch Leads Roll In

Once your ad is live, leads flow directly into your pipeline. Your automated follow-up texts and emails kick in instantly — all you have to do is show up and close the deals.
